About me

Postgraduate student at Balliol College, Oxford, and recent graduate from the University of Bristol.

I am currently reading for the MFoCS MSc at the Mathematical Insitute in the University of Oxford. Later this year I will begin study of a DPhil in Mathematics here, funded by a scholarship from the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research.

Before this, I completed my undergraduate education at the University of Bristol, where I earned a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science.

I am broadly interested in combinatorial and geometric group theory, as well as complexity theory and combinatorics.
